Ingredients:
One small can tomato paste
One normal can tomato sauce
Garlic powder
Italian seasoning
Black pepper

Steps:
1. Combine tomato sauce and paste in a small saucepan over low heat
2. Add garlic powder to taste (cover the surface of the sauce)
3. Add italian seasoning to taste (cover the surface of the sauce)
4. Add black pepper to taste (5-7 turns of the grinder)

Heat until warm to hot, being sure to stir to prevent burning or large bubbles,
as these may splash due to the thickness of the sauce.
